Beetroot & Red Onion Tarte Tatin

Description

A pastry bottomed tarte with beetroot as the main filling

Ingredients

  • Pre rolled puff pastry
  • Beetroots
  • Red onions
  • Brown sugar
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Rocket
  • Goats cheese

Method

  1. Wedge the beetroot, cut the onion smaller
  2. Marinade the vegetables in a mix of balsamic and brown sugar
  3. After some time, add the marinade mix to a skillet and cover with foil. Cook
  4. When the beetroot and onion is soft, smells great, remove from the heat and lay out the puff pastry covering the top. Tuck it in
  5. Put the whole thing in the oven for around 35 mins
  6. When the pastry is puffed and golden-brown, remove from oven and slide a knife round the edge to separate from the skillet
  7. Set a plate upside down on top of the tarte, making sure it's bigger that your creation. Flip so that the tarte ends up on the plate, remove skillet
  8. Crumble goats cheese and top with rocket
  9. Enjoy!
