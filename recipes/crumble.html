Peach & Basil Crumble

Description

This crumble defies expectations with a basil twist which adds a complexity to a familiar dish

Ingredients

  • Six peaches
  • Brown sugar
  • Basil leaves
  • Flour
  • Cold cubed butter

Method

  1. Turn your oven on to 180 degrees C
  2. Make crumble mix by rubbing the butter into the flour, let it form small breadcrumbs
  3. Cut your peaches into chunks however you want
  4. Blend basil with a little water so you can mix it with the peaches, if you are using canned peaches you can use the juice or syrup for blending
  5. Mix your basil blend with the chopped fruit and add to a pie tin. Add sugar at this stage if you feel necessary
  6. Spread the crumble mix evenly on the top of the crumble and sprinkle the sugar to taste
  7. Bake in the oven for 35-50 minutes, it should bubble and the crumble should become golden
  8. Cool before serving
  9. Enjoy!
